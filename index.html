Who We Are

Built to give
creatives a real voice.

die2harddie2fly is a collaborative creative ecosystem founded by Mendes Gabriel and Rodrigo Duarte. The project exists to support raw, underrepresented creative talent — providing structure, visibility, and access to professional-grade production, networks, and real opportunities. No industry mould. No compromise.

We bridge underground culture and the commercial world while keeping authenticity untouched. Right now we're a growing ecosystem. Long-term: a full agency signing models and artists, producing tailored content, and connecting culture to commerce.

How It Works

Our Process

01
Discover

We find raw talent without access. We bring them in, understand their vision, and build a plan around it — not around industry templates or safe expectations.

02
Curate

Project-based teams assembled for each production. The right creative directors, photographers, editors — chosen for the specific vision, not for generic availability.

03
Produce

Professional-grade production with authenticity intact. Photography, video, design — executed with obsessive craft and genuine respect for the creative's identity.

04
Amplify

Content distributed. Talent visible. Audience feedback filtered through creative direction — we evolve, improve impact, and increase reach while preserving who we are.
