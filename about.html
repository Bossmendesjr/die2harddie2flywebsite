About Die2Hard Die2Fly.

Raw · Authentic · Uncompromising

The Story

Built to give creatives
a real voice.

die2harddie2fly is a collaborative creative ecosystem directed by Mendes Gabriel and Daniel Ferreira. The project exists to support raw, underrepresented creative talent — providing structure, visibility, and access to professional-grade production, networks, and real opportunities. No industry mould. No compromise.

We bridge underground culture and the commercial world while keeping authenticity untouched. Right now: a growing ecosystem. Long-term: a full agency signing models and artists, producing tailored content, connecting culture to commerce.
